%%Control the audio volume.%%

%%Controls the audio volume of the stream being played. This has nothing to do with the system volume. This volume will be multiplied by the system volume. e.g.: if the current volume level is 0.5, and the system volume is 50%, it will be 0.5 * 0.5 = 0.25.%%{{page>:docs:efl:user:interface:efl:player:property:volume:description&nodate&nolink&nouser}}
