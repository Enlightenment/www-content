%%.ELM_WEB_ZOOM_MODE_AUTO_FILL will calculate the needed zoom level to fit the contents in the web object's size, without leaving any space unused.%%{{page>:docs:efl:user:class:elm:web:property:zoom_mode:getter_description&nodate&nolink&nouser}}
