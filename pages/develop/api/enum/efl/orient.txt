===== Description =====

%%An orientation type, to rotate visual objects.%%

%%Not to be confused with %%[[:develop:api:enum:efl:ui:dir|Efl.Ui.Dir]]%% which is meant for widgets, rather than images and canvases. This enum is used to rotate images, videos and the like.%%

%%See also %%[[:develop:api:interface:efl:orientation|Efl.Orientation]]%%.%%

===== Fields =====

{{page>:develop:api:user:enum:efl:orient:fields&nouser&nolink&nodate}}

  * **none** - %%Default, same as up%%
  * **up** - %%Orient up, do not rotate.%%
  * **right** - %%Orient right, rotate 90 degrees counter clock-wise.%%
  * **down** - %%Orient down, rotate 180 degrees.%%
  * **left** - %%Orient left, rotate 90 degrees clock-wise.%%
