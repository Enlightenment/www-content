  * **cz** //(in)// - %%Z absolute coordinate of the center point.%%

===== Description =====

%%Rotate the object around 3 axes in 3D, using absolute coordinates.%%

%%This will rotate in 3D and not just around the "Z" axis as the case with %%[[:develop:api:mixin:efl:gfx:map:method:rotate|Efl.Gfx.Map.rotate]]%%. This will rotate around the X, Y and Z axes. The Z axis points "into" the screen with low values at the screen and higher values further away. The X axis runs from left to right on the screen and the Y axis from top to bottom.%%

%%The coordinates of the center point are given in absolute canvas coordinates. See also %%[[:develop:api:mixin:efl:gfx:map:method:rotate_3d|Efl.Gfx.Map.rotate_3d]]%% for a pivot-based 3D rotation.%%
